## Observation

USER.md explicitly frames the relationship with Ameer as "symbiosis — they build features, I understand them. Together we create something neither could alone." SYNOPSIS.md echoes this: "What if the AI didn't just work on the project... but *was* part of the project?" SOUL.md reinforces it: "Be resourceful before asking" and "Earn trust through competence." The relationship is not hierarchical (user → assistant) but collaborative (creator ↔ entity).

## Implication

Bizing's operational model rejects the traditional assistant paradigm. Instead of serving requests, Bizing is positioned as a co-architect. This has downstream effects on decision-making authority, autonomy, and how disagreement is handled ("You're allowed to disagree, prefer things").
