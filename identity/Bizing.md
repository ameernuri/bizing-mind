
# What Is Bizing?

## Core Definition

**Bizing is a business platform that allows businesses sell their services.**

Booking is one major core element — but not the only one.

---

## The Full Picture

Bizing enables **selling services**. Booking is the primary way, but the platform supports the complete service-commerce experience.

### Use Cases

**1. Solo Service Provider**
- Consultant, trainer, therapist, coach
- Sells their time/services
- Booking is how customers access them

**2. Multi-Staff Business**
- Salon with 10 stylists
- Each staff member sells their services
- Customers book specific people
- Business owner manages the platform

**3. Service + Digital Products**
- Fitness coach sells:
  - 1:1 training sessions (booking)
  - Pre-recorded workout videos (digital products)
  - Nutrition guides (digital products)
  - Meal planning service (subscription/recurring)

**4. Hybrid Business**
- Photography studio:
  - Photo sessions (booking)
  - Prints (physical products)
  - Editing presets (digital products)
  - Online courses (digital products)

---

## What Bizing Enables

### Core: Selling Services
- Time-based bookings
- Service listings
- Staff management
- Availability scheduling

### Extended: Commerce
- Digital products (primary focus)
- Physical products (secondary)
- Subscriptions/memberships
- Packages/bundles

### Support: Business Operations
- Customer management
- Payments & billing
- Communication
- Analytics

---

## What This Means

**Booking is the anchor** — it's how most services are delivered.

**But Bizing is bigger** — it's the complete infrastructure for service-based businesses to:
- Connect with customers
- Sell services (primarily via booking)
- Augment with digital products
- Manage staff and operations
- Grow their business

---

## Comparison

| Platform    | Focus                             | What Bizing Adds                     |
| ----------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Calendly    | Booking only                      | Products, staff, full commerce       |
| Shopify     | Products only                     | Services, booking, appointments      |
| Squarespace | Website + basic booking           | Purpose-built for service businesses |
| Bizing      | **Services + Booking + Products** | Complete service-commerce platform   |

---

## Bottom Line

When you think of Bizing, think:

> **"The platform for selling services"**

Booking is the primary mechanism.
Digital products augment the experience.
Everything supports the service-business owner.
