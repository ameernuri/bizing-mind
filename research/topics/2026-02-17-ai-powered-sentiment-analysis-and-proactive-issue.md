**Status:** Proposed

## Description

Investigate how real-time sentiment analysis and predictive support capabilities can be integrated into booking platforms serving healthcare providers. Explore the specific application of voice and text-based emotional intelligence to detect patient frustration during appointment scheduling, combined with proactive interventions (e.g., offering callback support, suggesting alternative appointment times) to improve first-contact resolution and reduce no-shows in medical practices.

## Why This Matters

Healthcare businesses have unique requirements including HIPAA compliance, insurance verification, and complex appointment logistics.
