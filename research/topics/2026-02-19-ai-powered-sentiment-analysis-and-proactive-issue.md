**Status:** Proposed
**Created:** 2026-02-19
**Priority:** Medium

## Description

Investigate how AI sentiment analysis and predictive support capabilities can be integrated into booking platforms serving healthcare providers (clinics, therapists, dental practices). Explore how real-time emotion detection during customer interactions—combined with proactive issue prevention—can improve patient satisfaction, reduce no-shows, and enhance the pre-appointment experience. Focus on HIPAA-compliant implementations that maintain patient privacy while leveraging AI insights.
