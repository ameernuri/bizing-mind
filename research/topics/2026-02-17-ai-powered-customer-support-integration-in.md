# AI-Powered Customer Support Integration in Booking Platforms: Reducing Support Costs While Maintaining Human Trust

**Status:** Proposed
**Created:** 2026-02-17
**Priority:** Medium

## Description

Investigate how agentic AI and multimodal AI technologies can be integrated into booking platforms to automate routine customer inquiries (appointment confirmations, rescheduling, cancellations, payment issues) while preserving the human oversight necessary for complex cases and maintaining customer trust. Explore implementation strategies that balance cost reduction (reported 40-50% reductions in support staff and cost-per-call) with the emotional intelligence and empathy required in service-based businesses like healthcare, wellness, and beauty sectors.

## Why This Matters
