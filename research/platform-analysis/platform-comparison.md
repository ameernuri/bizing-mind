## Platform-Specific Strengths

### Calendly
**Best for:** Sales teams, consultants, recruiters, remote workers
- **Strengths:**
  - Easiest setup and use
  - 100+ integrations
  - Enterprise security (SSO, SCIM)
  - Workflow automation
  - Routing and qualification
- **Limitations:**
  - No class scheduling
  - Limited payment options
  - No POS integration
  - Basic client management

### Acuity Scheduling
**Best for:** Wellness, beauty, health professionals, therapists
- **Strengths:**
  - Comprehensive payment features
  - Package and subscription support
  - HIPAA compliance available
  - Intake forms and file uploads
  - Squarespace integration
- **Limitations:**
  - No free plan
  - No marketplace/discovery
  - Limited marketing tools
  - Requires Squarespace for full power

### SimplyBook.me
**Best for:** International businesses, complex needs, multi-industry
- **Strengths:**
  - 60+ modular features
  - 40+ payment gateways
  - Most customization options
  - Free custom website
  - Client mobile app
  - Multi-language support
- **Limitations:**
  - Complex pricing
  - Learning curve
  - No native video conferencing
  - Limited marketplace presence

### Square Appointments
**Best for:** Salons, barbershops, retail services, in-person businesses
- **Strengths:**
  - Integrated POS and payments
  - Free plan available
  - Tap to Pay on mobile
  - Square ecosystem (banking, payroll)
  - Waitlist feature
  - AI Assistant
- **Limitations:**
  - Limited customization
  - Square branding hard to remove
  - Fewer integrations
  - No class packages

### Mindbody
**Best for:** Fitness studios, gyms, yoga studios, multi-location wellness
- **Strengths:**
  - 3M+ consumer marketplace
  - Industry-leading for fitness
  - Comprehensive business management
  - Staff payroll and HR
  - Branded mobile app option
  - Strong retention tools
- **Limitations:**
  - High cost
  - Complex setup
  - Contract requirements
  - Overkill for small businesses
  - Must use their payment processor

| Platform | Entry Level | Mid Tier | Top Tier | Notes |

- Payment processing fees vary significantly

## Use Case Recommendations

### Choose Calendly if:
- You're a consultant, coach, or sales professional
- You need to schedule virtual meetings
- You want the easiest setup
- You need enterprise-grade security
- You use many different software tools

### Choose Acuity if:
- You're a wellness or health professional
- You sell packages or subscriptions
- You need HIPAA compliance
- You have a Squarespace website
- You need comprehensive intake forms

### Choose SimplyBook.me if:
- You need maximum flexibility
- You're outside the US (international payment gateways)
- You want a modular approach (pay for features you use)
- You need extensive customization
- You want a free custom website

### Choose Square if:
- You have a physical location
- You need integrated POS hardware
- You want simple, predictable pricing
- You're already using Square
- You need to process payments in-person

### Choose Mindbody if:
- You're a fitness studio or gym
- You want marketplace discovery (new client acquisition)
- You have multiple locations
- You need full business management (payroll, etc.)
- You can afford higher costs for more features

## Market Positioning

### By Business Size
- **Solo/Freelancer:** Calendly (free), Square (free), SimplyBook.me
- **Small Business (2-10):** Acuity, Square, Calendly Teams
- **Growing Business (10-50):** Acuity, Mindbody, SimplyBook.me
- **Enterprise (50+):** Mindbody, Calendly Enterprise

### By Industry
- **Sales/Consulting:** Calendly
- **Wellness/Health:** Acuity, Mindbody
- **Beauty/Salon:** Square, Acuity, Mindbody
- **Fitness:** Mindbody (leader), Acuity
- **Education/Coaching:** Acuity, Calendly
- **Medical/Healthcare:** Acuity (HIPAA), SimplyBook.me (HIPAA)
- **International:** SimplyBook.me

### By Primary Need
- **Ease of use:** Calendly, Square
- **Payment integration:** Square, Acuity
- **Discovery/marketplace:** Mindbody
- **Customization:** SimplyBook.me
- **Enterprise security:** Calendly
- **POS/hardware:** Square
- **All-in-one:** Mindbody

## Gaps in the Market (Opportunities)

1. **AI-powered scheduling** - None have advanced AI for optimization
2. **True marketplace** - Only Mindbody has significant consumer app
3. **Vertical-specific** - Most are horizontal; room for industry-specific platforms
4. **White-label** - Limited white-label options
5. **API-first** - Limited developer-centric platforms
6. **Bundle pricing** - Most don't offer true subscription bundles well
7. **Resource optimization** - Limited AI for resource allocation
8. **Predictive analytics** - Basic reporting across the board
