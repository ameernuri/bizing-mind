# Square Appointments - Complete Feature Analysis

## Overview
Square Appointments is an integrated scheduling and payment solution from Block, Inc. (formerly Square). It seamlessly combines booking management with Square's powerful POS and payment processing ecosystem, making it ideal for in-person service businesses.

---

## Core Scheduling Features

### Online Booking
- **Free booking website** - Customizable online booking site included
- **Website embedding** - Embed scheduler on existing sites
- **24/7 booking** - Always-available scheduling
- **Google integration** - Book directly from Google Search/Maps
- **Instagram integration** - Book from Instagram business profile
- **Facebook integration** - Booking on Facebook pages
- **Direct booking links** - Shareable URLs
- **Custom domains** - Use your own domain (Plus/Premium)

### Appointment Management
- **Unlimited calendars** - Any number of staff/locations
- **Multi-staff booking** - Multiple providers per appointment (Plus+)
- **Class bookings** - Group sessions and workshops
- **Recurring appointments** - Regular repeating bookings
- **Resource management** - Room, station, equipment tracking
- **Waitlist** - Automatic cancellation filling (Plus+)
- **Service categories** - Organized service menu
- **Service variations** - Duration and price options

### Availability Controls
- **Custom hours** - Set availability per day
- **Blackout dates** - Block time off
- **Buffer times** - Padding between appointments
- **Minimum lead time** - Advance booking requirements
- **Maximum advance booking** - How far ahead clients can book
- **Same-day booking cutoff** - Stop same-day appointments
- **Gap reduction** - Optimize schedule efficiency
- **Service-specific prep time** - Setup time per service

### Calendar Features
- **Real-time sync** - Instant updates across devices
- **Google Calendar sync** - Two-way synchronization
- **Color coding** - Visual organization
- **Drag-and-drop** - Easy rescheduling
- **Multiple views** - Day, week, month views

## Pricing Tiers

### Free ($0/month)
- Online booking site
- Unlimited calendars
- Appointment reminders
- Invoicing
- Square payment processing
- Customer directory
- **Processing fee:** 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction

### Plus ($29/month)
- Everything in Free
- Lower processing rates
- Cancellation policies and no-show fees
- Multi-staff appointment booking
- Waitlist
- Email and text message marketing
- Custom booking site domain
- **Processing fee:** 2.75% + 30¢ per transaction

### Premium ($69/month)
- Everything in Plus
- Lowest processing rates
- 24/7 support
- Future bookings report
- Service cost tracking
- Resource management
- **Processing fee:** 2.6% + 30¢ per transaction

### Custom Enterprise
- For businesses processing $250K+/year
- Custom processing rates
- Hardware discounts
- Onboarding support
- Technical specialists
- Account management

## Strengths
1. **Integrated payments** - Seamless POS and booking
2. **No monthly fee option** - Truly free plan available
3. **Square ecosystem** - Complete business platform
4. **Hardware integration** - Professional POS devices
5. **Competitive rates** - Lower processing on paid plans
6. **Waitlist feature** - Automatic cancellation filling
7. **AI Assistant** - Automated messaging

## Limitations
1. **Limited customization** - Less design flexibility
2. **Square branding** - Hard to fully remove
3. **No class packages** - Limited to appointments
4. **Basic marketing tools** - Compared to dedicated CRM
5. **No marketplace** - Less discovery than Mindbody
6. **Limited free plan** - Processing fees only, no advanced features

---

## Comparison to Other Platforms

### vs. Calendly
- Square: Better for in-person payments and POS
- Calendly: Better for virtual meetings and integrations

### vs. Acuity
- Square: Lower processing fees, better hardware
- Acuity: More customization, better for wellness

### vs. Mindbody
- Square: Lower cost, simpler setup
- Mindbody: Better for fitness, marketplace discovery
