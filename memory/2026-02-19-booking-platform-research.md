# Memory: 2026-02-19 - Booking Platform Research

## Research Completed

Conducted exhaustive competitive analysis of top 5 booking platforms, creating 6 comprehensive documents totaling ~70,000 words.

### Platforms Analyzed

1. **Calendly** - Scheduling automation for professionals and enterprises
2. **Acuity Scheduling** - Comprehensive service business scheduler (Squarespace)
3. **SimplyBook.me** - Modular system with 60+ features, international focus
4. **Square Appointments** - Integrated POS and booking for in-person services
5. **Mindbody** - Industry leader for fitness, beauty, and wellness with 3M+ consumer app

### Key Findings

#### Market Positioning
- **Calendly:** Best for sales teams, consultants, virtual meetings
- **Acuity:** Best for wellness, health professionals, subscriptions
- **SimplyBook.me:** Best for international, complex needs, customization
- **Square:** Best for in-person services, POS integration, retail
- **Mindbody:** Best for fitness studios, gyms, marketplace discovery

#### Pricing Tiers
| Platform | Free | Entry | Mid | Enterprise |
|----------|------|-------|-----|------------|
| Calendly | ✅ | $10/seat | $16/seat | $15k/yr |
| Acuity | ❌ | $16/mo | $27/mo | Custom |
| SimplyBook.me | ✅ | Variable | Variable | Custom |
| Square | ✅ | $29/mo | $69/mo | Custom |
| Mindbody | ❌ | ~$129/mo | ~$229/mo | Custom |

#### Feature Gaps Identified
1. AI-powered scheduling optimization
2. True marketplace (only Mindbody has significant consumer app)
3. Vertical-specific platforms
4. White-label solutions
5. API-first/developer-centric platforms
6. Advanced subscription bundling
7. Resource optimization AI
8. Predictive analytics

- Web fetch from official feature pages
- Pricing page analysis
